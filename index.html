    Artifact 1

  • Title:

    A Timeline of Artificial Intelligence

  • Introduction:

    Artificial Intelligence as a field was conceived as a philosophical idea before the 1940s, which then materialized into practical technology. This project documented the development of Artificial intelligence at each groundbreaking epoch from the 1940s to date.

  • Description:

    This artifact is flowchart that sequences the development of AI. Alan Turing, the pioneer of modern Artificial intelligence, started the most significant stage in the development process with the Turing Test in the 1950s. Subsequent developments are visualized in this flowchart to highlight the journey of AI advancement. The chart ends with the future of AI situating in General AI development—machines that can fully reason like human beings.

  • Objective:

    Primarily, this artifact enlightens stakeholders on the pedigree of Artificial Intelligence. Contrary to factions of public opinion that AI is ‘magic’, this artifact demonstrates how thorough and established Artificial Intelligence is. There are now several journal article publications that explore several aspects of Artificial Intelligence to advance the possibility of building an human-like machine. This artifact reveals this history behind AI.

  • Process:

    This artifact is a group project from the AIML-500 class. The group of four members collaborated to produce this artifact. The group brainstormed what sources we could utilize to find accurate information. We resolved to use sources like Google, Google Scholar, and ChatGPT. After identifying the relevant information we brainstormed how to convey this information as knowledge. The group decided to use Canva to design visualization for our information.

  • Tools/Technologies Used:

    Google Scholar, ChatGPT, Gemini, Google Canva

  • Value Proposition:

    This artifact is valuable to employees, and other stakeholders who want to understand the background of Artificial Intelligence.

  • References:

    Dr. Sandra Metzger, Aaryaneil, Marilyn, Quingyuan

    Artifact 2

  • Title:

    "Clements' Learning Assistant": Gemini AI chatbox

  • Introduction:

    Artificial Intelligence development continues to elevate the user experience with chatbots. Whereas, simple chatbots would have GPT technology that makes it possible to have meaningful exchanges with chatbots. Nonetheless, chatbots generally have custom settings that may affect user experience. For example, for a programmer, verboseness of code may be unwanted. Contrarily for a humanities expert, more information on subjects may be relevant. So, this artifact demonstrates how a Gemini AI chatbox is tuned to fit a specific communication goal.

  • Description:

    The Artificial Intelligence tool used in this project is Google’s Gemini—an advanced multimodal AI model. Gemini can process and generate text, image, audio, video and code. This chatbox is suitable for users of every category. The chatbox that is presented here is tuned to the following parameters:

  • - Be my expert tutor
  • - Provide references
  • - Quiz me and provide feedback
  • - Help me reflect on my learning

  • Objective:

    This project demonstrates the capability of an AI chatbox to guide an autonomous learning process for students.

  • Process:

    I selected the AI tool that I wanted to work with—Gemini. I selected the explore gems option, new gem. In this section I chose my chatbox name, then I inserted the parameters. After that I saved these settings and my Chatbox was ready.

  • Tools/Technologies Used:

    Gemini

  • Value Proposition:

    “Clements' Learning Assistant ” is an AI chatbox that facilitates an autonomous learning process for students and stakeholders. This chatbox makes sure to provide appropriate references to every information that the chatbox provides so that the AI generates only verifiable information. Also, the AI evaluates users on topics that the users seek to gain firm understanding. Finally, this AI chatbox guides users on a reflective process on knowledge obtained. Overall, this AI chatbox is streamlined to promote autonomous learning among users.

  • References:

    Dr. Sandra Metzger

    Artifact 3

  • Title:

    Simplifying the concept of Neural Networks

  • Introduction:

    In Artificial Intelligence, neural networks are a very important aspect of building intelligent machines. Modeled according to how neurons in the human brain function, artificial neural networks are complex mathematical structures that facilitate computer reasoning processes. Neural networks enable computers to learn from features of a dataset, then make predictions.

  • Description:

    This project seeks to explain what neural networks are by interacting with an AI tool—ChatGPT. Through this engagement, I try to ascertain the level of intelligence that the AI tool will display. Also, I seek to demonstrate the ability of this AI tool to learn from previous experiences.

  • Objective:

    The main objective of this project is to explain what neural networks are in the simplest form using ChatGPT as an AI resource.

  • Process:

    I accessed my account on OpenAI’s ChatGPT chatbox, then I inserted the prompt ‘what are neural networks’. Based on the responses, I queried the AI more to get specific responses that suit the goal of this project. I screen recorded this interaction, saved the video and then uploaded it on my Youtube channel.

  • Tools/Technologies Used:

    Apple Iphone, ChatGPT, Youtube

  • Value Proposition:

    This project is suitable for anyone who seeks to understand the building blocks of super intelligent machines that perform tasks such as facial recognition technology, autonomous vehicle driving, and image generation.

  • References:

    Dr. Sandra Metzger
